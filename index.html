How it works

We connect you - the buyer to verified real estate companies in Nigeria

1

Sign up and fill KYC form

Register and fill a detailed KYC form to enable us confirm your identity.

2

Prospect from our listings

Interact with our listings depending on your interest.

3

Agree to Terms

Subscriber and Property Company agree to secure a transaction via safeHouse Escrow (SafePay).

4

Payment to Escrow

Subscriber makes payment into SafePay, money is held in escrow until all obligations are fulfilled.

5

Receive Property Documents

Subscriber receives property documents via dedicated courier on due date as agreed.

6

Approval as show satisfaction

Subscriber confirms that all obligations have been fulfilled as expected by approving the transaction in their dashboard

7

Escrow release to Property Company

Funds are debited from the escrow and made availabe for withdrawal in the property company's account.
